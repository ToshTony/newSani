Why The SaniBook?

Revolutionize sanitation information: Turning every intervention into a documented success story for a healthier, cleaner Kenya.

The Critical Need: Unifying Invisible Solutions

Kenya's sanitation solutions are working, but they're invisible. Despite countless successful interventions, fragmented data and poor documentation prevent learning and coordination.

The **SaniBook Initiative** changes this by compiling these fragmented efforts into a unified, accessible **knowledge nexus**. This amplifies success and drives collective progress, ensuring that valuable, hard-won expertise informs national policy and future interventions.

Information Sharing Platform

A central digital hub for sharing validated success stories and best practices.

Shared National Work-plan

Facilitates coordination by identifying successful models for replication and scale.

Delivering Maximum Impact

Focuses resources on proven, effective hygiene and sanitation strategies.
