Meet our Silver Sponsor SBER at GITEX GLOBAL 2024


Russia’s largest bank and a leading global financial institution, Sberbank holds almost one-third of aggregate Russian banking sector assets. SBER is the key lender to the national economy and one of the biggest transaction handlers in Russia. 

The bank displays sustainability, stability and reliability of its services in the face of enormous demand and systematically strengthens its positions in the key segments of the banking market.

Over 112 million individual and corporate clients trust Sberbank today. On top of that, Sberbank is no longer just a financial institution, but a technology company with innovative solutions. These include AI solutions sought-after in education, healthcare, physical and cybersecurity, and other areas of life. The bank designs its products and processes, including research and development.

Listen to Maxim Eremenko, VP, Director of Dept. for Development of AI & ML share his insights on the offerings by SBER.

He further adds 'We appreciate the opportunity to tell the world about our AI solutions, share our experiences, and discuss the latest AI trends'

Meet their team at Hall 15, Stand C5 at GITEX GLOBAL 2024.
