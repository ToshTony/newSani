How you get to Expand North Star at Dubai Harbour


The world's largest startup event just got bigger. Taking over a new iconic venue at the new Dubai Harbour, Expand North Star will feature over 1,800 global startups, attract 900+ investors and feature a lineup of international tech geeks, VCs, unicorn founders and more. The fun does not stop here!

When business deals and conversations take a more casual turn, North Star by night facilitates even more networking in our hang out areas at Deck 1 and Deck 2. Think breath-taking Dubai skyline, drinks, food and live entertainment that has a different theme every day.

We want to make your journey to the Harbour as smooth as possible. Check out the best, cheapest and fastest ways to get to Dubai Harbour by public transport, car and taxi.
