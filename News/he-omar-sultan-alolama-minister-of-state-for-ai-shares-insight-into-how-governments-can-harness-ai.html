H.E. Omar Sultan AlOlama, Minister of State for AI, Shares Insight into How Governments can Harness AI


H.E. Omar Sultan AlOlama, Minister of State for AI, Digital Economy and Remote Work Applications at Government of the UAE, shares his message for the World on the UAE's plan for AI.

Gathering at the UNGA, he shared insights into how global governments can harness the power of AI and sent the UAE’s message to the world. A message that focuses on the commitment to leverage technology to create a global safeguard and a global watchdog that monitors the bad and combats it, and also promotes the good.
