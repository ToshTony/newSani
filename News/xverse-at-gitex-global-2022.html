X-VERSE at GITEX Global 2022


Virtual reality and gaming took centre stage at GITEX Global’s X-VERSE tent which displayed a variety of brands from the metaverse ecosystem

The global pioneers from the world of Web 3.0 were in Dubai at GITEX Global. Did you get a chance to see them?

Inspired by the Dubai Government’s vision and roadmap to become one of top 10 cities globally in the metaverse economy – the X-VERSE tent at GITEX displayed some of the world’s most advanced tech ecosystems, which has the capability to empower diverse industries within the metaverse.

To get a first-hand live environment experience of how diverse industries such as sports, gaming, fashion, music, retail, and art, among others will look in an increasingly vast and rich virtual system, visit the X-VERSE tent at GITEX Global.
