About Us

GITEX GLOBAL stands as the world's most established and iconic large-scale tech exhibition. For over four decades, it has been the premiere gateway for tech creators, investors and enthusiasts to collaborate. In 2025, the event scaled new heights, spanning across two mega venues - Dubai World Trade Centre & Dubai Harbour. It offered an unprecedented 40+ halls of exhibition space, showcasing tech giants and innovative startups in fields like AI, Cybersecurity, Mobility, Sustainable Tech and more.

And in 2026, the world’s largest tech show begins a bold new chapter. üìç 7–11 December 2026 | Expo City Dubai

Imagine five exhilarating days of conferences, live-action workshops, matched networking and next-gen business partnerships - all set against Dubai’s cooler December weather and vibrant peak season energy.

This move isn’t just about a bigger venue - it’s about a curated experience that blends top-tier networking with Dubai’s lifestyle, where innovation meets inspiration.

New dates. New home. Same unstoppable mission.

 

The GITEX GLOBAL ecosystem encompasses
9 co-located shows:

GITEX GLOBAL, AI Everything, GITEX Impact, Future Urbanism, GLOBAL Devslam, Expand North Star, Fintech Surge, Future Blockchain Summit, and Marketing Mania. Central to these shows are innovation, collaboration, and discovery. Attendees can explore real-world applications of AI and source cutting-edge innovations that reduce operating costs and enhance business efficiency.
