THE BOLDEST REINVENTION OF THE WORLD’S LARGEST TECH & STARTUP SHOW

GITEX GLOBAL 2026 will take place at Dubai Exhibition Centre, Expo City, with a Summit on 7th Dec and 4-day Expo from 8-11 Dec 2026

GITEX GLOBAL in 2026 launches into an entirely new dimension. A show already unmatched in scale and influence is being reimagined with new energy, new dates, and an iconic new home. It is a reinvention of the world’s largest tech, AI, and startup event into a frontier platform that accelerates the pace of global tech growth and the rising ambitions of AI economies worldwide.

Why Global Tech Giants Are All In on GITEX 2026

01

FIRST-EVER EDITION AT EXPO CITY A VENUE BUILT FOR GLOBAL TECH AMBITION

Host your brand at the world’s largest indoor events venue - 2.2M sq. ft. of prime space, part of a $2.7B expansion, with 20,000+ parking spots and seamless access beside the future world’s largest airport.

02

ALL-NEW, ALL-NEXT DESIGNED FOR MAXIMUM BUSINESS IMPACT

An all-new 5-day engagement: Kick off 7 Dec with the GITEX Scale Summit a full-day deep dive into the biggest tech trends, then continue with four days of Expo and high-value dealmaking from 8–11 Dec 2026.

03

THE ULTIMATE COLLISION OF GLOBAL TECH POWER

GITEX GLOBAL & Expand North Star reunite as one mega event where tech leaders, founders and investors connect, collaborate and shape the next big breakthrough.

BEYOND BUSINESS. TECH-CATION VIBES.
