Tech First Gulf’s Cutting-Edge AI-powered Solutions at GITEX GLOBAL 2025


With over 20 years of leadership in the IT distribution landscape, Tech First Gulf (TFG) stands at the forefront of digital innovation across the Middle East, Africa, and India. In this exclusive interview, Akashdeep Singh, Chief Strategy Officer of Tech First Gulf, delves into a strategy to bring cutting-edge AI-powered solutions that solve real business challenges, boosting efficiency, security, and collaboration across industries. 

What proprietary AI-powered technologies or platforms are you showcasing at GITEX GLOBAL, and how are these solutions transforming business operations or core industries?

At Tech First Gulf, our strategy is to bring cutting-edge AI-powered solutions that solve real business challenges—boosting efficiency, security, and collaboration across industries. At GITEX GLOBAL 2025, we’re proud to have vendors like Pure Storage, Commvault, Samsung, and Logitech on our stand. Pure Storage delivers AI-driven data management for smarter insights, Commvault automates data protection with AI, Samsung offers intelligent workplace devices, and Logitech enhances hybrid work with AI-powered collaboration tools. Together, these solutions are transforming how businesses operate in today’s digital world.

What role should governments and regulators play in building a secure, robust AI ecosystem that drives progress for businesses and individuals?

Governments have a unique responsibility—they need to be both enablers and protectors. On one hand, they should fuel innovation by supporting R&D, encouraging collaboration, and making data more accessible in a secure way. On the other hand, they must create guardrails that ensure AI is used ethically and responsibly. The challenge is keeping regulations flexible enough to evolve with the pace of innovation. We don’t want red tape slowing down progress, but we do need frameworks that build trust—for both businesses and citizens. It’s all about striking that right balance. 

How do you view the UAE’s position and initiatives in the global AI landscape compared to other leading nations such as the US, China, and the EU?

The UAE has taken a bold, proactive stance on AI—one that many larger nations are still trying to match. What sets the UAE apart is its execution. It’s not just about vision documents or strategies; it’s about real implementation. From appointing the world’s first Minister of AI to embedding AI across public services, smart infrastructure, and governance, the UAE is walking the talk. In many ways, it’s creating a model for how smaller nations can punch above their weight in the AI race—not by outspending, but by outthinking and outmoving.

 Can you tell us about your plans for GITEX GLOBAL 2025?

Yes, at GITEX GLOBAL 2025, we will announce new strategic alliances with cybersecurity and data platform vendors, expanding our portfolio to include partners from additional regions. Alongside this, we are launching a region-specific partner enablement initiative focused on enhancing cybersecurity maturity and readiness. This expansion reflects our commitment to delivering robust solutions and comprehensive support to partners across the MEA and beyond.

ABOUT THE COMPANY:

Tech First Gulf (TFG) is a value-added distributor and technology enabler, delivering advanced IT, AV, UCC infrastructure, ELV, Cybersecurity, and enterprise solutions across the Middle East, Africa, and India. With a robust portfolio spanning infrastructure, security, collaboration, cloud, and digital transformation, TFG empowers businesses through innovation-driven partnerships, deep market expertise, and strong vendor alliances.With over two decades of leadership in the distribution space, TFG brings a strategic go-to-market approach and seamless channel execution to ensure partners and customers stay competitive in a rapidly evolving digital economy. Headquartered in the UAE with a presence across multiple regional offices, TFG is committed to enabling digital acceleration and building future-ready businesses.
