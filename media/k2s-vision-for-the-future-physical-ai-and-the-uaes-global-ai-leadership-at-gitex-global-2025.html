K2’s Vision for the Future Physical AI and the UAE’s Global AI Leadership at GITEX GLOBAL 2025


At the forefront of redefining autonomous mobility, robotics, and human performance, K2 is emerging as one of the UAE’s most ambitious technology companies. With pioneering platforms such as AUTOgo, Autocraft, Eneron, and Team Kinetiz, the company is shaping the future of smart transportation, urban air mobility, and adaptive robotics. Waleed Alblooshi, Director of Corporate Communications at K2 shared valuable insights on how Physical AI and AIoT are transforming industries and why the UAE is uniquely positioned to bridge East and West in the global AI race.

How are Physical AI and AIoT (Artificial Intelligence of Things) revolutionizing industries and shaping societies around the globe?

Physical AI and AIoT are becoming the invisible infrastructure of modern society ,reshaping mobility, security, agriculture, energy, and healthcare. At K2, we see AI not as a back-office tool, but as the nervous system of a smarter world. From autonomous vehicles that navigate without human input to humanoid robots that adapt to real-world environments, we’re witnessing a shift from passive automation to responsive intelligence embedded in the physical world. 

How can governments and regulators contribute to the development of a safe, strong AI ecosystem that propels advancement for both individuals and companies?

Governments must be both enablers and guardians. The balance lies in fostering open experimentation while creating guardrails that protect rights, data, and safety. K2 advocates for national AI strategies that combine regulatory clarity with investment in local talent, IP development, and cross-border collaboration, particularly in sectors like autonomous mobility and robotics, where real-world deployment requires policy alignment.

In comparison to other prominent countries like the US, China, and the EU, how do you see the UAE's role and initiatives in the global AI landscape?

The UAE has positioned itself as a neutral, agile innovation hub — uniquely capable of bridging East and West. While the US and China dominate in scale, and the EU leads in governance, the UAE stands out for its speed, clarity of vision, and national alignment around AI. Initiatives like Smart Mobility mandates, and G42’s global partnerships prove that the UAE is not just participating — it's setting the pace.

What proprietary AI-powered technologies or platforms are you showcasing at GITEX GLOBAL, and how are these solutions transforming business operations or core industries?

K2 will showcase several flagship platforms at GITEX 2025: AUTOgo Nova: Our next-generation autonomous robotaxi for smart city mobility; Eneron Magnus:  Next Generation Patrol Vehicle; Autogo Humanoid - a breakthrough in adaptive human-robot interaction; and OTM Coffee Machine - an autonomous coffee barista “ Serving Coffee.”

What features of GITEX GLOBAL most interest or excite you, and what role does participation play for your organization?

GITEX is where vision meets velocity. It gives us a global platform to demonstrate how the UAE is not only adopting emerging technologies — but shaping them. For K2, it's the ideal moment to connect with investors, regulators, and future collaborators who believe in building the physical world of AI — not just the digital one.

ABOUT THE COMPANY:

K2 is a UAE-headquartered technology company shaping the future of autonomous mobility, robotics, aviation, and human performance. K2 is home to pioneering platforms such as AUTOgo (autonomous systems, transportation), Autocraft (urban air mobility), Eneron (mobility solutions platform specialising in electric and hybrid special-purpose vehicles), and Team Kinetiz (autonomous racing team).
