Korean AI Platform Developer Signs MOU at Dubai's GITEX GLOBAL, Eyes Market Expansion in India and Middle East


F1Soft Ink Partners with Indian Tech Firm LAAYN Technologies to Expand AI Manufacturing Solutions in Middle East and South Asia

Korean AI Platform Developer Signs MOU at Dubai's GITEX GLOBAL, Eyes Market Expansion in India and Middle East

DUBAI, October 17, 2025 — F1Soft Co., Ltd. (CEO Oh Seung-ho), a South Korean AI solutions provider, announced today that it has signed a memorandum of understanding (MOU) with India-based LAAYN Technologies at GITEX GLOBAL in Dubai, marking a strategic expansion into emerging markets.

The partnership, formalized at one of the world's largest technology exhibitions, aims to combine F1Soft's expertise in AI-driven public sector solutions with LAAYN's capabilities in industrial automation and smart city infrastructure.

F1Soft has been a key participant in South Korea's "Inter-Ministerial Cooperation-Based AI Expansion Project" since 2023, an initiative led by the Ministry of Science and ICT and the National IT Industry Promotion Agency (NIPA). The company has been developing an AI-powered platform for the Korea Fair Trade Commission to automate the review of unfair contract terms—a project that has demonstrated the practical application of AI in streamlining government operations.

Under the new agreement, the two companies will collaborate on advanced manufacturing digital transformation (DX) technologies, including AI-based smart factory systems, cloud manufacturing execution systems (MES), data analytics platforms, and digital twin solutions.

Bridging Korean and Indian Tech Ecosystems

LAAYN Technologies brings extensive experience in artificial intelligence, data engineering, cloud infrastructure, DevOps, and enterprise resource planning (ERP) and customer relationship management (CRM) systems. The Indian firm has a proven track record in executing smart city and industrial automation projects across South Asia.

This collaboration is expected to accelerate F1Soft's international expansion strategy, particularly targeting the manufacturing sector that is rapidly adopting Industry 4.0 technologies.[3][6] The partnership will leverage F1Soft's AI algorithms and platform architecture alongside LAAYN's implementation capabilities and regional client networks.

By combining their technological expertise and networks, the two companies plan to jointly execute AI smart factory implementation consulting and construction projects across India, the Middle East, and Southeast Asian markets,[2][5] supporting digital transformation (DX) initiatives for regional manufacturing companies.

The partnership positions both firms to capitalize on the growing demand for smart manufacturing solutions in emerging markets where governments and industries are investing heavily in technological modernization and industrial digitalization.

"This partnership represents more than a commercial agreement—it's a bridge between two innovative tech ecosystems," said Oh Seung-ho, CEO of F1Soft. "By combining our AI platform expertise with LAAYN's regional market knowledge and infrastructure capabilities, we're positioned to deliver next-generation manufacturing solutions to industries across India and the Middle East."

Strategic Positioning in Growing Markets

Industry analysts note that both India and the Middle East represent high-growth markets for AI-driven manufacturing solutions. India's manufacturing sector is undergoing significant digitalization under the government's "Make in India" initiative, while Gulf Cooperation Council (GCC) countries are investing heavily in diversifying their economies through technology adoption.

F1Soft's involvement in South Korea's government-backed AI expansion program has provided the company with valuable experience in developing scalable, reliable AI systems for critical applications—expertise that could prove valuable in markets with similar infrastructure development needs.

The company plans to use this partnership as a springboard for establishing a broader foothold in South Asian and Middle Eastern markets, with the ultimate goal of building a global supply chain for AI-powered manufacturing solutions.

About the Companies

F1Soft specializes in AI platform development and has been recognized for its work in public sector digital transformation. The company's participation in the Korean government's inter-ministerial AI expansion project has positioned it as a leader in applying advanced AI technologies to real-world administrative challenges.

LAAYN Technologies is an India-based technology services company offering comprehensive solutions in artificial intelligence, cloud computing, data engineering, and enterprise systems integration, with particular strength in smart city and industrial automation projects.

This partnership announcement comes at a time when Korea is expanding AI adoption across both public and private sectors through government-backed initiatives, solidifying its position as a global AI innovation hub—a development that heightens expectations.
